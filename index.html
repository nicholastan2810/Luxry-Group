Anti Fund

ANTI FUNDÂ® is a venture capital firm founded by Jake Paul and Geoffrey Woo in 2021.

Anti Fund believes the best founders are rebels, and the "anti" ethos resonates with the best engineers, scientists, and creators. Startups live and die on two levers: capital and attention. While capital is commodity, commanding attention is not. Whether a company sells consumer products or is a foundational research lab, they all must compete on the ability to win mindshare and ultimately customers and revenues.

Venture capital returns are driven by iconoclasts who win. Anti Fund's mission is to find and fund these people.

Select investments

Anti Fund focuses on early-stage investments and advisory in AI; robotics; software and high-growth consumer companies with differentiated technology and/or distribution advantages. Anti Fund believes that companies that boldly challenge incumbents and break traditional paradigms are also the venture investments that generate the best financial returns.

FAQ

What stage do you focus on?

We tend to invest at pre-seed or seed where we can maximize participation and support the inflection of the business.

Will you invest in late-stage rounds?

If we believe in a 10x+ return profile and are excited about the product/market, we may consider it.

What do you invest in?

We invest in high-growth AI, robotics, SaaS, and consumer companies with tech/distribution advantages.

What's your check size?

We invest between $250K-$500K, but have written checks as small as $50K and checks as large as $3M+. We are comfortable both leading and participating.

How can I invest in the fund?

We're not raising right now, but do reach out to ir@antifund.com if you're interested in future funds.
